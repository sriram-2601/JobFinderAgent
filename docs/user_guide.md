Welcome to the Autonomous AI Job Search & Application Platform. This manual details the operational mechanics, matching configurations, and agent workflows.

## 2. Operational Modes
You can toggle between two modes on the dashboard side-panel:

### Mode 1: APPROVAL MODE (Default)
1. **Discovery**: Newly crawled jobs are saved.
2. **Analysis**: AI Matcher scores the job.
3. **Alerts**: Telegram / Email informs you of a matched job (Score >= 70).
4. **Approval**: The job enters `PENDING_REVIEW` state.
5. **Execution**: Playwright only applies after you click **Approve** on the dashboard.

### Mode 2: AUTONOMOUS MODE
1. **Discovery**: Newly crawled jobs are saved.
2. **Analysis**: AI Matcher scores the job.
3. **Execution**: If Score >= 80, the Auto-Applier immediately launches and submits the application.
4. **Log**: Screenshots and confirmations are saved automatically.

---

## 3. Experience and Role Filtering Rules
To protect candidate integrity, the system applies strict entry-level filters:
- **Maximum Experience**: Excludes any job description asking for more than 2 years of experience.
- **Senior Exclusions**: Instantly skips titles containing: *senior, lead, principal, architect, manager, director, staff, sr.*
- **Eligible Employment Types**: Search covers Full-Time, Internship, Part-Time, Contract, Associate, and Graduate Trainee roles.
- **Eligible Work Modes**: Remote, Hybrid, and Onsite.

---

## 4. Match Scoring & Priority Boosts
The AI Matcher evaluates the job description using Gemini 2.5 Flash. It computes a base matching score (0-100) and then adds programmatic boosts to prioritize target areas:
- **+20 Boost**: AI, Generative AI, LLM, Machine Learning, and Python roles.
- **+15 Boost**: Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, and Full Stack roles.
- **+10 Boost**: React, Node.js, Cloud (AWS/DevOps/SRE), Testing (QA), and IT Support.

*If the final score is less than 70, the job is rejected. Score 70-79 is routed to manual review. Score 80+ is routed to auto-apply.*

---

## 5. Playwright Form Filler Automation
The Auto-Applier reads forms, identifies applicant portals (Lever, Greenhouse, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, etc.), and uploads files:
- **Resume Upload**: Automatically locates file attachments and uploads `Sriram_Resume.pdf`.
- **Cover Letter**: Pastes a cover letter pre-generated by Gemini tailored to the company's stack.
- **Screening Questions**: Answering is performed by Gemini based on Sriram's resume content.
  - **Low Confidence Safeguard**: If Gemini's answering confidence is below 80%, the system registers a Telegram question alert, pauses, and awaits your input via the dashboard or bot command (`/ans <job_id> <answer>`) before continuing the submission.

---

## 6. Outreach & Skill Gap Analyzers
- **Referral Finder**: For matching jobs (score >= 85), the agent finds LinkedIn URLs of recruiters or engineering managers at that company and drafts Outreach and Follow-up templates.
- **Skill Gap Analyzer**: Processes all Crawled job descriptions to compile a list of trending technologies, identifies what is missing from Sriram's resume, and drafts a Weekly Learning Roadmap.
- **Interview Tracker**: Connects via IMAP to scan email inbox. It auto-updates the status of your applications when it detects coding test requests (OAs), interview invitations, or offers.
